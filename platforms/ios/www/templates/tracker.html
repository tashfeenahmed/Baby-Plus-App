Baby+ Tracker

Day 37th

The implanted cluster of cells is now called an embryo and is smaller than a grain of rice.





Day 38th

The implanted embryo has started to produce hormones that may register on a home pregnancy test, yielding a positive result. For some women, the test will not register as positive for another week.





Day 39th

The growing embryo is still only about the size of an apple seed and is shaped like a small tadpole.





Day 40th

Within the embryo, the heart is starting to divide into chambers and tiny cardiac cells are beginning to contract.
