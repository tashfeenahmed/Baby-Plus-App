Tip of the Day

DAY 37TH

Anemia is common in Pakistani women. Take your multivitamins and icon suppliments properly





DAY 38TH

Don't lie flat on your back, particularly after 16 weeks, because the weight of your bump presses on the main blood vessel bringing blood back to your heart and this can make you feel faint





DAY 39TH

Folic acid is crucial for the proper development of the baby's neural tube (it covers the spinal cord), and it's vital for the creation of new red blood cells.





DAY 40TH

Even everyday tasks like scrubbing the bathroom or cleaning up after pets can become risky when you're pregnant.
